DIGIX INTERNSHIP

Digix, one of the world’s largest and fastest-growing online platforms for digital solutions, offers an exceptional opportunity for undergraduate students through its annual 10-week paid Summer Internship program at our Bangkok office. This program is meticulously designed to provide ambitious students with the chance to work in a world-class, collaborative environment and deliver real business results. Prepare yourself for a rewarding summer experience unlike any other at Digix!

Meaningful Work, Global Impact

As an Agoda intern, you get to exercise your creativity and execute your ideas to reach millions of Agoda customers and partners around the globe. From strategy to technology, no matter which team is your home, you get the chance to collaborate on projects that matter and help travelers experience the world.

Rich Learning Opportunities

Join as a student, leave as a professional. Throughout the 10-week internship program, you will get the chance to attend workshops conducted by Agodans that will equip you with the skills to kick-start your career. By the end of the program, you will also present a business case to a panel of executives.

Truly International Environment

Accelerate your career through hands-on work experience alongside the brightest minds of more than 90 nationalities from top-tier global companies. Our flat structure allows us to stay agile, move fast, and take real ownership – bringing you along on our exciting journey to build travel for the world.
